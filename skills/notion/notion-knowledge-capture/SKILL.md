# Notion Knowledge Capture

This skill enables Manus to transform conversations, discussions, and insights into structured documentation in your Notion workspace. It captures knowledge from chat context, formats it appropriately, and saves it to the right location with proper organization and linking.

## Capabilities

- **Context Analysis**: Identifying key insights, decisions, and action items from discussions.
- **Structured Formatting**: Converting raw conversation into professional documentation (FAQs, How-to guides, Decision logs).
- **Notion Integration**: Saving content directly to Notion wikis, databases, or pages.

## Bundled Resources

- **`reference/`**: Database schemas and best practices for Notion organization.
- **`examples/`**: Templates for different types of documentation.
- **`evaluations/`**: Sample inputs and expected outputs for quality assurance.
