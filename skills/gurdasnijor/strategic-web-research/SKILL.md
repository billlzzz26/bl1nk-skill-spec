# Strategic Web Research

Get comprehensive, well-planned research results by combining strategic thinking with neural search. The skill breaks down complex queries, searches intelligently, and synthesizes findings.

## Capabilities

- **Plans research strategy**: Using Sequential Thinking to break down queries.
- **Searches intelligently**: Using neural search to understand concepts.
- **Structures findings**: Clear organization of discovered information.
- **Synthesizes information**: Providing a unified perspective with proper attribution.

## Use Cases

- Market research and trend analysis.
- Competitive intelligence gathering.
- Fact-checking and verification of claims.
- Gathering technical documentation or research papers.
- Summarizing complex topics or news events.

## Bundled Resources

- **`research_framework.py`**: Utilities for organizing and structuring research.
- **`synthesis_framework.md`**: Methodology for synthesizing findings.
